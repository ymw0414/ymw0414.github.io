**Abstract.** This paper studies how local exposure to NAFTA-era trade shocks shaped U.S. newspaper coverage of trade policy. Combining a large corpus of local news articles with a shift-share research design, I measure changes in media slant on trade-related issues and examine their downstream effect on political attitudes and voting behavior.
