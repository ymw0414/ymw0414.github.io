Hi, I'm **Minwoo Yoo** — a Ph.D. Candidate in Economics at The George Washington University.

I work at the intersection of **media economics**, **labor economics**, and **applied microeconometrics**. My research uses causal inference methods — difference-in-differences, instrumental variables, and shift-share designs — combined with text-as-data and machine learning, to study how economic shocks shape political and media discourse, as well as labor market outcomes.
